operations analytics case study

Capital Bikeshare Operations

Demand and rebalancing analysis to improve fleet availability across stations and time windows.

Duration
2025
Skills
Spatial-Temporal EDA, Forecasting, Demand Segmentation, Operations Planning

Overview / Description

Analyzed station-level trips, temporal demand shifts, and weather effects to identify where shortages and surpluses formed throughout the day.

Objective / Success Metrics

Improve bike and dock availability during peak windows while minimizing rebalancing miles and overtime requirements.

Approach

Combined usage clustering with short-horizon demand estimates and proposed route bundles for redistribution teams.

Results / Key Insights

Commute-driven directional imbalances dominated weekday patterns, while weekend demand was more leisure-centric and weather-sensitive.

Impact / Conclusion / Future Work

The approach can support shift planning and dynamic routing; future work includes integrating live dock telemetry for intraday optimization.
