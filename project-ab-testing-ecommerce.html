experimentation case study

A/B Testing E-commerce Behavior

Measured whether image reviews causally improved conversion across product cohorts.

Duration
Spring 2025
Skills
Experiment Design, Hypothesis Testing, Power Analysis, Causal Inference

Overview / Description

This project evaluated the business impact of user-generated image reviews by comparing treatment and control user journeys.

Objective / Success Metrics

Estimate conversion lift with statistical confidence and define rollout thresholds balancing expected gain with implementation cost.

Approach

Designed randomized assignment, monitored guardrail metrics, and performed segment-level significance checks to verify consistency.

Results / Key Insights

Image reviews showed positive conversion effects in trust-sensitive categories, with weaker impact in low-consideration purchases.

Impact / Conclusion / Future Work

Recommended staged rollout by category and further testing on review prominence and mobile placement to optimize lift.
