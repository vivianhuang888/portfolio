Hi, I’m Vivian, a data analyst with a strong interest in healthcare analytics and solving real-world problems. I’m especially interested in using data to uncover insights that improve efficiency, identify opportunities, and support better decision-making. With a dual graduate background in Health Administration (MHA) and Business Analytics (MSBA), I focus on using data to support better decision-making.
