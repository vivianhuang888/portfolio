payer analytics case study

Predicting Preventive Care Engagement

Risk stratification for Medicare members to improve targeted outreach and care gap closure.

Duration
2025
Skills
LightGBM, Feature Engineering, Healthcare Segmentation, Model Evaluation

Overview / Description

The project modeled preventive care participation likelihood to support outreach prioritization under constrained care management resources.

Objective / Success Metrics

Increase engagement among high-risk members while improving precision of outreach lists and reducing low-yield contact volume.

Approach

Developed a LightGBM model with calibration checks, then translated model scores into operational outreach tiers with transparent business rules.

Results / Key Insights

The highest-risk tier captured a disproportionate share of future care gaps, enabling more focused intervention planning for care teams.

Impact / Conclusion / Future Work

This framework supports routine campaign planning; future iterations can include social vulnerability features for equity-sensitive prioritization.
