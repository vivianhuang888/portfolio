Predicting Preventive Care Engagement

A risk-stratification model to prioritize outreach for Medicare members with open preventive care gaps.

Duration
2025
Skills
Python, LightGBM, Feature Engineering, Model Calibration, Healthcare Segmentation

Overview / Description

Care teams often have limited outreach capacity and need a reliable way to prioritize members most likely to benefit from engagement.

This project predicted preventive care participation propensity and translated model scores into practical outreach tiers for operations teams.

Objective / Success Metrics

The objective was to improve campaign efficiency without sacrificing equity in member outreach:

  1. Precision: Increase concentration of open care gaps in top-priority outreach lists.
  2. Efficiency: Reduce low-yield contact attempts.
  3. Usability: Provide interpretable score bands for care managers.

Approach

1) Data preparation

Combined claims history, utilization patterns, and demographic features to build member-level training records.

  • Engineered recency/frequency indicators for preventive service behavior.
  • Handled class imbalance to preserve high-risk signal detection.

2) Modeling and validation

  • Model: LightGBM classifier tuned for ranking performance.
  • Validation: Calibration and lift checks across operational segments.

3) Operational translation

Mapped predicted risk into outreach tiers with simple business rules aligned to staffing capacity.

Results / Key Insights

The top outreach tier captured a disproportionate share of future care-gap closures, improving campaign targeting quality.

Primary outcomeHigher lift

Operational outputTiered outreach

Score transparency made adoption easier for care management teams.

Impact / Conclusion / Future Work

The framework supports repeatable preventive campaign planning with clearer resource allocation and measurable follow-up outcomes.

  • Impact: Improved targeting efficiency for preventive outreach operations.
  • Future work: Incorporate social determinants to strengthen equity-aware prioritization.
