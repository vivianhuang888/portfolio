consulting case study

Digital Growth & Market Strategy

Structured growth recommendations for an organic F&B SME through the IXL Accelerator.

Duration
Fall 2024
Skills
Market Sizing, Value Chain Analysis, Unit Economics, GTM Strategy

Overview / Description

This engagement focused on helping a scaling SME clarify product priorities and reduce operational friction across sourcing, packaging, and channel planning.

Objective / Success Metrics

Define a data-backed expansion roadmap with clear milestones for margin improvement, channel fit, and launch readiness.

Approach

Mapped end-to-end value chain costs, profiled customer segments, and tested alternative pricing and distribution scenarios with leadership stakeholders.

Results / Key Insights

The final recommendation narrowed focus to highest-contribution SKUs and sequenced channel expansion to protect cash flow and execution quality.

Impact / Conclusion / Future Work

The team adopted a phased rollout plan with KPI checkpoints; future work includes CRM instrumentation and retention cohort analysis.
