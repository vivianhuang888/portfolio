business analytics case study

Yelp Customer Review Analytics

A cloud-enabled BI workflow to connect sentiment and operational attributes to engagement trends.

Duration
Summer 2025
Skills
SQL, BigQuery, Tableau, Sentiment Analysis, Dashboard Design

Overview / Description

The project unified large-scale Yelp review and business metadata to identify what factors most influenced ratings and customer retention behavior.

Objective / Success Metrics

Deliver a stakeholder dashboard that tracks rating movement, review velocity, and feature-level sentiment for decision support.

Approach

Created a BigQuery data model, engineered reusable SQL views, and published Tableau dashboards with filters by cuisine, location, and service attributes.

Results / Key Insights

Response latency, review recency, and consistency indicators showed stronger relationships to rating trajectory than single-attribute amenities alone.

Impact / Conclusion / Future Work

The workflow enables repeatable monthly reporting; future work can integrate competitor benchmarks and review text topic drift monitoring.
