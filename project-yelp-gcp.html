business analytics case study

Yelp Customer Review Analytics on GCP

A cloud BI pipeline linking review sentiment, operations attributes, and rating trajectory.

Duration
Summer 2025
Skills
Google BigQuery, SQL, Tableau, Sentiment Analysis, Dashboard Design

Overview / Description

Local businesses receive large volumes of reviews, but turning that text into clear operational decisions is difficult.

This project built a cloud-native analytics workflow to connect review patterns with service attributes and identify factors most associated with rating momentum.

Objective / Success Metrics

The objective was to deliver a reusable reporting layer for business and marketing stakeholders:

  1. Clarity: Track sentiment and rating changes by market and category.
  2. Speed: Reduce manual reporting through scheduled SQL views.
  3. Actionability: Highlight operational levers tied to customer perception.

Approach

1) Data foundation

Loaded and modeled Yelp review and business tables in BigQuery with cleaned dimensions for geography, category, and service features.

  • Built reusable aggregation views for trend analysis.
  • Created sentiment buckets for easy stakeholder interpretation.

2) Analysis layer

  • Exploration: Measured review velocity, recency, and sentiment mix.
  • Visualization: Delivered Tableau dashboards with drill-down filters.

3) Decision support

Packaged insights into weekly KPI summaries for operations and customer-experience teams.

Results / Key Insights

Review recency and management responsiveness were stronger drivers of rating trends than single amenity features alone.

Top signalResponse latency

Delivery formatLive dashboard

The dashboard made it easier to prioritize operational fixes with measurable customer sentiment impact.

Impact / Conclusion / Future Work

The project established a scalable GCP analytics pattern for recurring review intelligence and stakeholder reporting.

  • Impact: Improved decision speed by centralizing fragmented review metrics.
  • Future work: Add topic modeling and competitor benchmarking for deeper market context.
